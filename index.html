About This Feed

This feed provides real-time OCGN stock data including:

  • Regular trading hours prices
  • Extended hours (pre-market and after-hours) data
  • Open, High, Low, Close prices
  • Trading volume

The feed is automatically updated every 15 minutes during market hours and beyond.

How to Use

To subscribe to this feed in your favorite RSS/Atom reader, use this URL:
